# How to Generate an Image in Canva

## Step 1: Open Canva

Go to [www.canva.com](https://www.canva.com) and sign in using your school gmail account.

Create a new A4 design by clicking **create a design** in the top right and searching for "Document (A4 Portrait)".

## Step 2: Go to Elements and scroll down to find "Generate Your Own"

Click on that to bring up the Generative AI menu!

## Step 3: Enter your prompt, choose a style, choose a ratio, and press generate!

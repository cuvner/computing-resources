# Lesson 1 – What is a "Computer" anyway?

## In this lesson

In this lesson, we'll look at defining what a "Computer" actually is, including the role of data and instructions in making a computer work.

## Learning Outcomes

- I can identify the role of instructions and data in making a Computer work.
- I can identify the order of file-size measures from Bytes to Terabytes.

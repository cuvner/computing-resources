# Lesson 4 – Hazards and Risks in Cybersecurity

## In this lesson

We'll be looking at the threats that businesses face from cybercriminals, and how businesses keep their networks secure.

## Learning Outcomes

- I can explain how two types of cyber attack that I have studied work.
- I can summarise and explain the three main consequences to a business of cyber attacks.
- I can identify the main types of cybersecurity risks facing a business.

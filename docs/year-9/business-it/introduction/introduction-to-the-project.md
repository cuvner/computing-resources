# Introduction to the Project

## In this unit

You're going to take on the role of a Project Manager for a media company. Your job is to plan how the company will set up computers and networks in a brand-new office.

You'll learn how to choose the right computer for different jobs, build a network to connect everything, and understand the main online threats businesses face. You'll also work out how to keep your company safe from hackers and other risks—while sticking to a budget.

This unit is all about solving real-world problems using IT.

## The Project – Planning a New Office for Phoenix Media

![Project lifecycle diagram](https://lwfiles.mycourse.app/6313816770e7826017236062-public/1369cf2f838b17bea4e3ae875844c26e.png)

Every big project goes through different stages. These are called the Project Lifecycle. You can see the five phases in the diagram above:

- **Initiate** – getting the project started and identifying what you need to do
- **Plan** – working out all the details you need to consider
- **Execute** – putting the plan into action
- **Monitor and control** – checking everything is going to plan
- **Close** – finishing the project and reviewing how it went

In this unit, you'll be focusing on **Phase 2: Plan**.

Your challenge is to plan how your media company will set up a brand-new office. You'll think about what computers are needed, how they'll connect to each other, and how to keep the company's data safe. You'll make real decisions, just like a project manager in a business would.

![Workplace skills](https://lwfiles.mycourse.app/6313816770e7826017236062-public/7a6523cddc0edbd8cb5666a73d90fe0c.png)

As part of this, you'll be using skills that are really important in the workplace—like budgeting, writing a risk assessment, and creating a company policy to help keep your business secure.

## Intro to Phoenix Media

![Phoenix Media](https://lwfiles.mycourse.app/6313816770e7826017236062-public/90597c2710a937c056cdfd9b71611444.jpg)

Phoenix Media started out as a small video production company based in Manchester. At first, they worked on local projects—like filming events and editing short adverts for local businesses. But over the last few years, they've grown fast. Now they create digital content for companies all over the UK, including music videos, social media campaigns, podcasts, and live streams.

Now, Phoenix Media is opening a new satellite office in London. They've chosen you to take on the role of Project Manager to help plan the IT setup. Your job is to make sure this new office has the right equipment, a working network, and protection from online threats—all while sticking to a budget.

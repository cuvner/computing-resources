# Lesson 5 – Mitigating Cybersecurity Risks

## In this lesson

We'll be looking at the threats that businesses face from cybercriminals, and how businesses keep their networks secure.

## Learning Outcomes

- I can research common cybersecurity risk controls.
- I can explain the definition of hacking, and give some examples of cyber-related crimes.
- I can complete a cybersecurity risk assessment, identifying appropriate risk-reduction controls.

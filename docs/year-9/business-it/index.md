# Business IT

*Year 9 · 5 lessons + project introduction*

You'll take on the role of a Project Manager for Phoenix Media, planning how the company will set up computers and networks in a brand-new office. You'll learn how to choose the right computer for different jobs, build a network to connect everything, and understand the main online threats businesses face — all while sticking to a budget.

## Lessons

0. **[Introduction to the Project](introduction/introduction-to-the-project.md)** — the project lifecycle; meet Phoenix Media
1. **[What is a "Computer" anyway?](lesson-1/lesson-1-what-is-a-computer-anyway.md)** — instructions, data, and file sizes from bytes to terabytes
2. **[How to Buy The Right Computer](lesson-2/lesson-2-how-to-buy-the-right-computer.md)** — CPU, RAM, storage, and GPU; choosing computers within budget
3. **[Computer Networks & The Internet](lesson-3/lesson-3-computer-networks-and-the-internet.md)** — LANs, WANs, network hardware, and how the internet works
4. **[Hazards and Risks in Cybersecurity](lesson-4/lesson-4-hazards-and-risks-in-cybersecurity.md)** — types of cyberattack and their consequences for a business
5. **[Mitigating Cybersecurity Risks](lesson-5/lesson-5-mitigating-cybersecurity-risks.md)** — hacking, defences, and completing a risk assessment

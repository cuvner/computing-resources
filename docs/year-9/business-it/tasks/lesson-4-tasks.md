## Task: Cybersecurity Risk Assessment

**Step 1**

Make a copy of the example risk assessment [here](https://docs.google.com/document/d/13oWychmrs1ydEdva1A7CNb73afMl0gh9/edit).

**Step 2**

For each of the types of cyberattack you have studied, identify the risks to the business, the employees, and the business's customers.

**Step 3**

Assess how likely the risk is to happen, and how damaging it would be if it did. You do **not** need to complete the control measures yet — we will do that next lesson.

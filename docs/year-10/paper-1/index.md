# GCSE Paper 1 Theory: Principles of Computer Science

*Year 10 · Edexcel GCSE Computer Science — Paper 1 theory*

This unit covers the theory content for Edexcel GCSE Computer Science Paper 1: how computer systems are built, the difference between general purpose and embedded systems, and how the CPU processes instructions.

## Lessons

1. **[Intro to Computer Systems](lesson-1/lesson-1-intro-to-computer-systems.md)** — general purpose vs embedded systems, input/process/output, components of a computer, and the advantages of embedded systems
2. **[Inside the CPU](lesson-2/lesson-2-inside-the-cpu.md)** — the Control Unit, ALU, buses, registers, and the clock

Only Lessons 1 and 2 have been migrated so far — more lessons from this unit are coming soon.
